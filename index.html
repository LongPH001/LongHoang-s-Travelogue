Macau is a special land owned by the SAR (Special Administrative Region of China), heavily influenced by the Portugese settlement back in 1500's.

During my trip to Macau, I traveled from Hong Kong via a long bus ride across an impressive bridge spanning the sea. The architectural brilliance of the bridge left me in awe, especially as it transitioned into an underwater tunnel deep beneath the sea. Spanning a remarkable 50 kilometers, this engineering marvel was truly unforgettable.
